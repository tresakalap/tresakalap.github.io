Administration of the Province

The Provincial aided by her Council administers the Province under the authority of the Superior General and in conformity to the Rule of Life. Being responsible for the animation of the Province, she along with her council makes sure that all the Sisters live according to the spirit of the Gospel and objectives of the Congregation by providing adequate means to develop and enhance personal resources by organizing seminars, professional training and personality development sessions and also spiritual growth through sessions and retreats.
